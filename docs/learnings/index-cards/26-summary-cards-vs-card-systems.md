# Summary Cards Vs Card Systems

A summary card is just a repeatable content block for one item, while a card
system is a broader reusable UI pattern with shared components, variants, and
styling rules.

Why it matters here:
In Phase 6.2, a "card" can simply mean a clear summary block for one topic,
article, or action. You do not need a full reusable card architecture just to
render topic discovery content well.

Rule of thumb:
Start with plain repeated summary blocks. Extract a reusable card component only
after the markup clearly repeats and the shape has stabilized.
