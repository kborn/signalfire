# Information Architecture

Primary site structure:

- Home
- Topics
- Actions
- Articles
- Events
- Submit
- About

Each section links to related content across the platform.

Phase 11.5 public identity rules:

- The public product name is `Find Your Fight`.
- Home should orient visitors toward Topics as the starting point for choosing
  an issue, with routes into Articles, Actions, and Events for understanding
  and participation.
- About explains the product philosophy of focused participation without
  introducing additional product capabilities.
