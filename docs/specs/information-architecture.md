# Information Architecture

Primary site structure:

- Home
- Issues (`/topics`)
- Actions
- Articles
- Events
- Submit
- About

Each section links to related content across the platform.

Phase 11.5 public identity rules:

- The public product name is `Find Your Fight`.
- Home should orient visitors toward Issues as the public starting point for
  choosing a focus. The underlying route and data model remain `topics`, with
  routes into Articles, Actions, and Events for understanding and
  participation.
- About explains the product philosophy of focused participation without
  introducing additional product capabilities.
